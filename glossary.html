Neptune Glossary

Simple explanations of important Neptune Privacy terms.

zk-STARKs

Zero-knowledge proofs that allow Neptune to verify transactions without revealing private details.

Mutator Sets

A privacy structure that lets Neptune track outputs without revealing ownership.

Triton VM

The private smart contract engine used by Neptune.

Tail Emission

A continuous small reward to miners that keeps the network secure long-term.
