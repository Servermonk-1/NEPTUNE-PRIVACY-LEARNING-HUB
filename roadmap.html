Neptune Journey

Roadmap

Our path to quantum-safe privacy blockchain — from concept to global adoption.

Phase 1: Foundation (2023-2024)

Research and development of core cryptographic primitives. Invented Mutator Sets and Triton VM architecture. Established quantum-resistant foundations with hash-based proofs.

  • zk-STARKs integration for privacy proofs
  • Mutator Sets for private UTXO tracking
  • Triton VM design for confidential smart contracts

Phase 2: Testnet Launch (2025)

Public testnet deployment and community testing. Fair token distribution through mining. No presale, no VC funding.

  • Testnet launch: November 2025
  • XNT mining begins
  • Community governance framework
  • Wallet and explorer development

Phase 3: Mainnet & Ecosystem (2026)

Mainnet launch with full privacy features. Ecosystem expansion with dApps, exchanges, and partnerships.

  • Mainnet launch: Q2 2026
  • DEX integration with Triton VM
  • ✅ MEXC Exchange listing — April 2026
  • ✅ SafeTrade listing — completed
  • Developer tools and SDKs

Mass adoption through enterprise solutions and consumer applications. Cross-chain interoperability.

  • Decentralized identity integration

Phase 5: Quantum Era Leadership (2029+)

Leading the quantum computing era with advanced privacy features and scalable infrastructure.

  • Advanced zero-knowledge applications
  • Scalable privacy protocols
  • Research partnerships with universities
  • Continuous protocol upgrades
